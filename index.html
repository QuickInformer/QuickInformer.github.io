The Gospel Message:
Redemption through Jesus Christ


Hope in Jesus Christ

"For God so loved the world, that He gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in Him should not perish but have everlasting life." -John 3:16

The Human Condition

In the beginning, humanity was separated from God because of sin. People tried to make things right through animal sacrifices, but these sacrifices could only temporarily cover sin and couldn't fully restore the broken relationship between humans and God. -Leviticus 4:35

God's Plan of Redemption

However, God had a plan to provide a permanent solution. Out of His great love for humanity, He sent His Son, Jesus Christ, to Earth. Jesus, who is both fully divine as the Son of God and fully human, lived a perfect and sinless life among us. He showed us God's love, taught us about God's kingdom, and demonstrated His power through miracles. -Isaiah 53:3-5

The Sacrificial Death of Jesus

But the most amazing part of Jesus' mission was His sacrificial death on the cross. Jesus willingly gave His life as the perfect sacrifice for the sins of humanity. His death fulfilled the purpose of all the animal sacrifices once and for all. Through His sacrifice, Jesus offered forgiveness and reconciliation with God to all who believe in Him. -Romans 5:8

The Resurrection and Victory

Three days after His death, Jesus rose from the dead, demonstrating His victory over sin and death. This resurrection confirmed His identity as the Son of God and the power of His sacrifice. Because of Jesus' sacrifice and resurrection, people can now have their sins completely forgiven and their relationship with God fully restored. -Romans 6:9

Faith and Salvation

Now, anyone who believes in Jesus and accepts Him as their only Lord and Savior can experience the good news of the Gospel. They can have a personal relationship with God, receive forgiveness for their sins, and have the hope of eternal life. Jesus' sacrifice was enough to cover all sins, past, present, and future, making animal sacrifices obsolete. -Ephesians 2:8-9

The Gospel's Promise and Purpose

In summary, the good news of the Gospel is that through Jesus Christ, God offers forgiveness, reconciliation, and eternal life to all who believe in His Son. Jesus is the Son of God, the perfect sacrifice for sin, and the only way to salvation. His death and resurrection provide the way for humanity to be saved and restored to a right relationship with God. The Word of God Almighty, also known as the "Bible", states, "Jesus said unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by Me." -John 14:6

God is Almighty, amen. There is nothing impossible for Him. Trust in His loving-kindness.

Steps Towards Deliverance and Spiritual Growth

Below are some key procedures necessary for your experience with the Holy Spirit of God:

  • Repentance of sin
  • Believe in Jesus
  • Follow His teachings
  • Personal relationship with God
  • Seek guidance
  • Serve and worship Him
  • Connect with others
  • Share faith
  • Grow spiritually
  • Trust in His grace
